About

I grew up in Lake Tahoe, studied 'Business Management Economics' at UC Santa Cruz, and moved to New York in 2003 to work on Wall Street. After 7 years of every financial position imaginable, I felt I reached a peak and wanted a new challenge. In 2010, I moved back West to learn how to code. Since having moved back West, I've built several websites and have worked on several web development projects for a number of companies.

In 2015, I had my first child and it really changed the way I looked at the world. In 2017 I took a major step out of my comfort zone and decided to give back to my community by becamoming a Middle School teacher in East Oakland. I teach Social Studies, English, and am also teaching underserved youth how to code. In my free time, I work on my modest side business called 'The Sneaker Savant' - born from my childhood hobby of collecting things.
